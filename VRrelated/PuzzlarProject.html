MY DESIGN IN THE PUZZLER PROJECT

This is a puzzler project with magic ambience. In this game, you will be invited to a chamber, which is full of medieval and magical atmosphere. Without addressing the puzzler, you won’t be able to get out. The puzzler is simple. There will be 5 balls in the chamber lighted up in certain sequence after you getting in. What you have to do is to remember the sequence, and redo it. Sounds as hints will help you to address it. I hope the player enjoys this puzzler.

Iteration & User Testing

Version 1

1. Set up the chamber and 5 magic balls

2. Design the UI Panels, including Start Panel and End Panel

3. Add the light and the sounds

User test 1(Feedbacks)

1. It looks like in a mid-century castle with a historic feeling. The environment is quiet and with the lights down I feel comfortable. It stimulates me to explore this chamber and find something interesting. I get more and more excited!

2. The height of me is a little bit weird, it seems that I only a little taller than the barrels. I think I should be taller? Unless I am a child or a Gnome :P/laughing/

3. Both of the two panels are quite close to my face, I feel I can almost hit them.

Version 2

1. Add movement from the begin point to the playing point, and also to the end point.

2. Adjust the distance from the player camera to the two panels respectively and make the Camera higher.

3. Add the Terrain and the ambience sounds.

4. Rotate the direction light to make it darker.

User test 2(Feedbacks)

1. It’s better to change the style of panels and balls to fit the castle style. Now the distances to the panels are OK.

2. The movement feels good.

3. The inside room music is a little horrifying.

Version 3

1. Change the material of panels and balls.

2. Add the main part of the game function with lighting up the balls in sequence and allowing the player to follow it.

User test 3(Feedbacks)

1. In the begin panel, it is better to add game instructions to let the user know how to play the game.

2. The overall feeling of the game is good.

Conclusion

The frame of the game is almost complete. It has created an medieval and magical atmosphere. In this game, the player can learn how to play in the beginning. He/she will use VR to address the puzzler, and has chance to experience the movement. It will be a good opportunity to step into a new world with immersive feeling.

Next steps

1. Making the game more complex and with diversified puzzlers.

2. Adding some particle system to improve the immersive experience.

3. Improving the movement style to create a different experience.
